About
=====

OmniMirror is designed for artists and technical artists who need symmetry tools that remain stable after a model becomes more complex than a simple mirror modifier workflow can handle.

Blender already provides many excellent modeling tools, but production meshes often carry data beyond vertex positions: UV islands, vertex groups, corrective shapes, facial expressions, and left/right naming conventions. When those assets drift out of symmetry, fixing each layer manually can be slow and error-prone.

OmniMirror focuses on a reusable symmetry configuration. Once the add-on knows which vertices correspond across the mirror plane, it can use that knowledge consistently across multiple operations:

* Mesh symmetrization with average, forward, and reverse modes.
* Vertex group symmetrization for single groups or left/right group pairs.
* Shape key symmetrization and splitting.
* UV side preview, UV symmetrization, and rigid UV snapping tools.

The goal is to make symmetry cleanup feel deliberate rather than mysterious: configure once, inspect what was found, then apply the result where it matters.
